# I Need Help\!

It’s okay if you’re stuck\! We’re here to help you advance your hardware skills. You’re part of a greater community where you can ask for help.

## I need help with my project

Don’t know what part to use? Don’t know what a part does? Need help wiring?

Check our [Helpful Resources](/docs/resources/helpful-resources) section. We’ve compiled a list of tips and tricks to help you.

Try searching on Google first. You can find datasheets which contain specs for parts, Reddit, StackExchange and forums have lots of information. ChatGPT can also lead you into the right direction, but you should confirm what it recommends\!

Ask the community on Slack. We have thousands of teenagers like you on our Slack (Discord-like chatroom) who are interested in hardware\! Ask in the [\#fallout](https://hackclub.enterprise.slack.com/archives/C037157AL30), [\#hardware](https://hackclub.enterprise.slack.com/archives/C6C026NHJ), or [\#electronics](https://hackclub.enterprise.slack.com/archives/C056AMWSFKJ) channels. Be specific about your task and be kind. Other people aren’t going to design your project for you.

## Fallout is confusing. The site is broken

Don’t get the submission process, or how to begin? Is something on the site broken?

Ask the Fallout organizers on Slack. Reach out in [\#fallout-help](https://hackclub.enterprise.slack.com/archives/C0ACJ290090) on our Slack (Discord-like chatroom) for support. Be specific about what’s confusing, or broken. Keep in mind that we have a lot of requests to go through, so be patient.

## I’m having trouble joining Slack

The Hack Club community uses Slack (Discord-like chatroom, popular with more professional workspaces). You can join Slack by verifying your account through the box to the right of the Path, or through [hackclub.com/slack](https://hackclub.com/slack/). You should join the [\#fallout](https://hackclub.enterprise.slack.com/archives/C037157AL30) channel ([here’s how](https://slack.com/help/articles/205239967-Join-a-channel)).

If you’re struggling to join our Slack, or something isn’t working there. You can reach out to us at [fallout@hackclub.com](mailto:fallout@hackclub.com).
