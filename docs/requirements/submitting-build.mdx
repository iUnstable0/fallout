# Submitting your Build

<aside class="callout callout-info">

If you haven’t submitted your design, [do that first](/docs/requirements/submitting-design)\!

</aside>

Once you’ve built your project IRL, your koi fish turn to \[COMING SOON\]. You can now use those towards travel, parts to iterate your project, or other prizes (which will be released after the IRL event)\!

As a reminder, Fallout wants you to create [**real, shipped**](/docs/requirements/what-is-shipping) projects.

Similar to submitting your design, we have a list of **minimum requirements**, missing any of these will automatically get your project returned.

**It’s easy to miss one, so please read through all of it.**

<p class="text-xl! text-center text-red-600">
  <strong>95% of Rejections come from problems that take 5 Minutes to fix.</strong>
</p>

We’re here to help you, but we can’t tell you exactly what to do. Please read our documentation, ask the community, Google it, before reaching out to us.

## Make sure you’re meeting the requirements to submit your design

<aside class="callout callout-alert">

Submitting your build still requires you to meet all the requirements of submitting for design. You can find those [here](/docs/requirements/submitting-design).

</aside>

## Show your project, now built IRL

At minimum, you should include:

- [x] Pictures of your project, finished and IRL
- [x] A demo video showing it off and how it works. Share it on YouTube or Reddit\!

## Update your magazine page

At minimum, it should now include:

- [x] Include a QR code to your demo video. Make sure the QR code works\!
