# How to Qualify for Fallout

It's simple:

**Spend 60 hours designing or building your project, [tracked](/docs/project-resources/how-to-timelapse), get a ticket to Fallout.**

Your project needs to be approved by our team. Make sure you meet out submission requirements for [submitting design](/docs/requirements/submitting-design) or [submitting build](/docs/requirements/submitting-build) and submit your project for review by the deadline. We're reviewing projects on a rolling basis, so the sooner you submit, the better!

## What's included in the ticket?

For the event days (July 1-7), **all for free**, we'll be covering all your food, lodging, and transportation within Shenzhen. We'll also have materials for your project, plan activities and conduct workshops for you, at no cost for you.

Transportation to and from Shenzhen, China (flights, trains, etc.) is not included in the ticket. We have travel stipends available to help offset some of the costs, which you can read about in our [Travel Stipends](/docs/travel/travel-stipends) page.

You'll also be able to spend your [koi](/docs/project-resources/project-funding) (earned from designing and building your project) to redeem accommodation for the day before & after the event in our shop, in case you flight arrives earlier, or leaves later.

Otherwise, if you decide to come early or stay later, you'll be responsible for yourself, your own lodging and food during that time.
