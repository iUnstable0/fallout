# Travel Stipends

We're a nonprofit running on a limited budget, but we get that travel to China can be expensive! We’re offering travel grants to offset some of the cost.

**For every approved hour you spend, you earn 7 Koi. Once you build your project, the leftover Koi from that project becomes Gold. 1 Koi → 1 Gold.**

You will be able to redeem Gold for travel stipends at:

- **7 Gold → $8 USD ($8 for every hour of work)**

We have more info on how to track your time over in our [How to Timelapse](/docs/project-resources/how-to-timelapse) page.

<aside class="callout callout-info">

By reducing the amount you spend on project parts, you can gain more travel stipends.

</aside>

Flight prices can vary and increase as we approach summer. Booking your flight early is an option, but please consider the risk of not qualifying to Fallout and not getting your project approved. If you booked your flight and then don't qualify or don't get travel stipends, we unfortunately won't be able to reimburse you for the flight or allow you into the event.

## Need-based stipends

We've set a pool of funds aside to offering merit-based need-based stipends to participants a case-by-case basis, up to $1500 USD per person (subject to increase).

<aside class="callout callout-info">

**We'll be releasing the form to closer to the event date.**

</aside>

If cost is a barrier to your participation, and the above travel stipends aren't enough to cover your costs, consider requesting a need-based stipend. We will prioritize those who have built high quality projects.

Do note that this is a limited fund, and we may not be able to offer stipends to everyone who applies. Spending hours to get travel stipends is the best way, but we encourage you to apply for a need-based stipend if you think you need it. We will do our best to support as many people as we can!
